My Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe



What you'll need:

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Obtain two pieces of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on bread A, side A, with knife
  3. Spread jelly on bread B, side A, with knife
  4. Throw knife away
  5. Push two bread A and B together so that sides A are touching
  6. Consume
