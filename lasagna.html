My Fancy Lasagna Recipe



What you'll need:

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara
  • More Cheese
  • Ground meat

Directions

  1. Cook ground meat in skillet until done
  2. Mix ground meat with marinara
  3. Cook lasagna noodles partially in some boiling water
  4. Put some meat sauce on bottom of dish
  5. Top with cheese
  6. Layer with lasagna noodles
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 two more times
  8. Top with Cheese
  9. Bake at 350F for 1.5 hours
  10. Enjoy
